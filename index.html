Bruno's exclusive story by Bobby & Fiby

Bobby and Fiby adopted Bruno quite recently. They had been thinking about it for a while: “ Welcoming a(n old) human in a family ain't a light decision, you never know how he might react.” mutters Bobby. Fiby tempers “...Bruno should still have a great deal of years ahead of him...and he does not lack enthusiasm!”. Bruno loves to learn they say and now that he is well potty trained, they have enrolled him on a 4-months Java developper course. They like the idea that a human might eventually be able to code and, who knows, one day, become intelligent.
